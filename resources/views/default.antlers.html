Application Information

To obtain an application please email SPFArtistServices@LakeWorthBeachfl.gov. In the email please notate if you are an individual artist or with a school.

After receiving the application. Please print out the application and fill it out completely and legibly.

  • Attach a sketch or photo of your proposed street painting.
  • Attach two different examples that are representative of the artwork that you create.

Return the completed application via email to: SPFArtistServices@LakeWorthBeachfl.gov Space is limited.

We’re here to help

The Street Painting Festival is not a competition. The selection committee has a very difficult time processing all of the potential applicants, so be sure you qualify by making certain that your application is filled out completely and legibly and with all of the required attachments.

The 28th Annual Street Painting Festival is February 26 & 27, 2022. Looking forward to hearing from you! Deadline for Application is January 10, 2021.

Lake Worth Beach

Street Painting Festival

Over 600 artists use the pavement as canvas to transform downtown lake worth into a temporary outdoor museum of original art and masterpiece reproductions. The tradition of street painting traced back to 16th century italy when itinerant artists would use their chalks to transform pavement into a makeshift canvas, street painting has retained its appeal through the centuries. As in ages past, crowds gather to watch as fine works of art emerge. The “paintings” last only until the next rain, but the lively spirit and accessibility of the exhibition captivates audiences and inspires lasting memories.
